About Qanba

Qanba was founded in 2009 with a mission to create the finest gaming peripherals. Our manufacturing headquarters is located in ShenZhen, China. We design and produce our own arcade stick parts and arcade buttons. We have created various peripherals over the years, and are most known for our joysticks. Our joysticks are used worldwide by casual gamers to champions of fighting game tournaments.

FAQ

Please visit our shipping/returns page for policy information.

Each Sony Licensed Qanba product that you purchase directly through us or one of our authorized retailers has a one year warranty from date of purchase. All other products have a warranty of 90 days from date of purchase.

Please contact the retailer that you purchased your Qanba product from directly. In the case that they cannot assist you, we will be able to offer replacement parts or unit if you purchased from one of our authorized retailers. Due to legal reasons, we cannot assist with product purchased outside of authorized retailers.
